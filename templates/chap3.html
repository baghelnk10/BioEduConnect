Objectives & Outcomes

This section would outline the goals of the learning module and what learners should be able to achieve by the end. It could also include a brief overview of the topics covered.

Learn More
×
  1. You should be able to learn the three main practices for improving crop yield viz., crop variety improvement, crop production improvement and crop protection management.
  2. You should be able to hold discussion on how one can bring about improvement in food resources.
  3. You should be able to understand how Biotic and Abiotic factors affect crop production.
  4. You should be able to learn about the different desirable agronomic characteristics.
  5. You should be able to differentiate between manure and fertilizers.
  6. You should be able to understand the importance of irrigation and following specific cropping pattern.
